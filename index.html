Rules (high-level)

Full rulebook is posted in Discord for members.
League settings (gameplay + realism) +

Competitive mode • 6-minute quarters • Salary cap ON • Coaches • 72-hour advance. The goal is realistic roster-building, fair competition, and clean scheduling.

Scheduling + activity enforcement +

Schedule within 24 hours of week opening. Respond within 24 hours when tagged. Strike system: warning → probation (no trades) → replacement. Force-win requests require screenshots in tickets.

Trades + anti-collusion +

Trade approvals are required. Tier-based trade limits apply each season. No trade-backs, rentals, or collusion. Staff can reverse or veto for league integrity.

Salary cap + restructures +

Restructures are allowed but limited and tracked. The purpose is to keep cap realism and prevent cap exploits from warping multi-season balance.

Rule of thumb

If it looks like it breaks realism, it probably does. Staff rulings are final so the league stays stable across multiple seasons.
